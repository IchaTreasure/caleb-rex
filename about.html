About

Caleb Rex is an anointed multi-talented gospel artist from Rivers state who broke fearlessly into the gospel scene in 2016 with his unique debut single “Good morning”. He’s a pastor and music director in the Revelation ministries Inc.Caleb has performed at the various events in Nigeria.

He discovered the talents of singing and writing as a teenager and decided to nurture them. At secondary school, he learnt to play the drum, piano and guitar, three instruments he is quite passionate about. His musical influences include Tim Godfrey, Tye Tribett, Sammy Okposo and other great artists. His approach to song-writing and his powerful voice are some of the things that stand him out.
